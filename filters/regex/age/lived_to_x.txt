# Regular expression to identify age of the a deceased patient in the notes
# Looks for numeric values either single or double digits following the words lived to or LIVED TO in the notes
\b(?<=lived to\s|LIVED TO\s)(***REMOVED***9***REMOVED******REMOVED***0-9***REMOVED***|***REMOVED***1***REMOVED******REMOVED***0-9***REMOVED******REMOVED***0-9***REMOVED***)\b
