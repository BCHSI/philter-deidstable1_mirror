# Regex to find 10 digit phone number separated by - 
# This also captures cases where the last 4 digits are x or *
(\b\d(\-|\s|\.))?(\b\d{3}\s?***REMOVED***\s\-\.***REMOVED***?\s?|\(\d{3}\)***REMOVED***\s\-\.***REMOVED***?\s?)?\b(***REMOVED***a-zA-Z***REMOVED***)?\d{3}***REMOVED***\s\-\.***REMOVED***(\d{3,4}|xxxx|xxx|\*\*\*\*|\*\*\*)(\/\d{4})?
